# Workforce Insights: Employee Performance & Attrition Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project presents an **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze employee performance, job satisfaction, and attrition patterns within an organization.

The dashboard helps HR teams understand workforce trends, identify potential attrition risks, and evaluate employee satisfaction and performance.

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights several important workforce metrics:

* **Total Employees:** 1470
* **Average Job Satisfaction:** 2.73
* **Average Performance Rating:** 3.15
* **Average Monthly Income:** 6.50K
* **Attrition Rate:** 0.16

These KPIs provide a quick overview of employee engagement and organizational performance.

---

##  Dashboard Visualizations

###  Age vs Years at Company

A scatter plot showing the relationship between **employee age and years spent at the company**, helping identify experience distribution across different age groups.

### 2️ Attrition by Department

A bar chart analyzing employee attrition across departments:

* Research & Development
* Sales
* Human Resources

This helps HR teams understand which departments experience higher employee turnover.

### 3️ Performance Rating by Job Satisfaction

This visualization compares **employee performance ratings with job satisfaction levels**, providing insight into how satisfaction may influence performance.

### 4️ Employee Count by Gender

A pie chart showing gender distribution of employees within the organization.

---

##  Interactive Filters (Slicers)

The dashboard allows users to filter the data using:

* **Marital Status**
* **Job Role**
* **OverTime**
* **Other HR attributes**

These slicers allow deeper exploration of workforce data.
